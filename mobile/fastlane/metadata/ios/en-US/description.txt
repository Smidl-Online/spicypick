SpicyPick is a daily social game that challenges your moral compass. Every day, face a new morally ambiguous scenario and cast your verdict: Guilty, Not Guilty, It's Complicated, or Both Wrong.

HOW IT WORKS
Each day brings a fresh real-life-inspired scenario. Read the situation, make your call, and see how the community voted. Compare your verdict with expert analysis and earn XP for your picks.

KEY FEATURES
- Daily Scenarios: A new moral dilemma every day to keep you thinking
- Community Verdicts: See how thousands of other players voted
- Expert Analysis: Get professional perspectives on each scenario
- Leagues & Rankings: Compete weekly, climb the ranks, earn promotions
- Streaks & XP: Build daily streaks, level up, and unlock achievements
- Challenge Friends: Send scenarios to friends and compare verdicts
- Themed Packs: Explore curated scenario collections by topic
- Submit Your Own: Write your own morally ambiguous scenarios for the community
